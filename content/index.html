Welcome!

I'm Micah Ernst, Pastor of Grace Lutheran Church, and I invite you to share in the truth of the gospel at Grace Lutheran Church in Columbia, Missouri.

Whether you are new to the teachings of Jesus or a long time follower of the Christian faith, we pray that the Holy Sprit may bless you with new or renewed faith through the good news of our Savior who assures you of forgiveness through his death and resurrection.

Grace Lutheran is privileged to proclaim the pure gospel in accordance with the main teachings set forth in the Lutheran Confessions. Grace Lutheran is a member of the Wisconsin Evangelical Lutheran Synod.
